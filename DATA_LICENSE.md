1. **Platform Terms of Service** — you must comply with the ToS of the platforms from which data were collected
2. **Attribution** — please cite this project and contact shannon@loyal9.app before using data for publication
3. **Usage**: Research and validation only. Commercial or production use requires express permission and formal ethics review
